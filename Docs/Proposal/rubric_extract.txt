Project  Proposal  Evaluation  Rubric  (20  Points  Total)
 
Your  proposal  should  answer  the  following  questions:  
●  Introduction:  What  is  the  broad  context  of  your  project?  Why  does  it  matter?  ●  Background/Literature  Review:  What  prior  work  exists?  How  does  your  proposal  
relate
 
to
 
or
 
differ
 
from
 
it?
 ●  Research  Questions:  What  problem  are  you  solving?  Why  is  it  interesting?  ●  Data:  What  dataset  will  you  use?  How  large  is  it?  What  attributes  does  it  contain?  ●  Approach:  What  methods,  algorithms,  or  techniques  will  you  use?  ●  Evaluation:  How  will  you  measure  success?  What  baselines  exist?  ●  Expected  Outputs:  What  do  you  expect  the  final  results  to  be?  ●  References:  What  sources  informed  your  proposal?
